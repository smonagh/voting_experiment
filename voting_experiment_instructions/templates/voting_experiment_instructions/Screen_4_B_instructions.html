After your counterpart decided which message to send, you will see the following on your screen:

You will then be asked to choose either option A or option B. Your choice will determine the payments in the experiment. You will never know what sums and probabilities were actually offered in the project not chosen. Moreover, you will never know the sums your counterpart could be paid with the other project. We will pay the two of you according to the choice YOU make.
