The purpose of this experiment is to learn about decision making by individuals.

In this experiment you can earn money which you will be paid in cash at the end of this experiment.

How much money you will earn will depend on your decisions and the decisions of the other participants in this experiment. By making some decision but not others, you will make choices that are either likely to increase your earnings, or are likely to decrease your earnings. These instructions explain the kind of decisions you can make. To earn money, it is important that you understand these instructions.

In this experiment, each participant can earn money. How much money you will earn will depend on

  1. Only the decision that you make
  2. Only the decisions that other participants make
  3. The decision that you make, the decisions that other participants make, and who among the three Type A participants in a given round is randomly selected to earn the money that is associated with the project that the Type B participant chose to implement

We are interested in your decisions, that is, the decisions that you make on your own. This also means, from here on please no talking. Please turn off all electronic devices. If you have any questions during the experiment, or if you any trouble with the computer, please raise your hand. We will come to you to answer your questions.

For cash payment purposes, you will be assigned a random identification number (ID).

The following pages will explain the experiment in detail. The last pages of this explanation contain short questions to test your understanding of these instructions. You and the other participants must answer each question correctly before the experiment can start.

The experiment consists of 18 decision-making rounds.

Each round provides you with the potential to earn money, which is expressed in experimental dollars, E$. At the end of this experiment, your earnings in experimental dollars which will be converted to US dollars, US$, and your earnings in US$ will be paid to you. The conversion rate is 1 US dollar for 6 experimental dollars.

At the end of this experiment, a summary screen will display your total earnings in US$.

Please sit quietly after the experiment has concluded, and wait to be called to receive your earnings. You will receive your earnings in cash and privately, Being paid privately means that the other participants cannot observe how much money you earned.
